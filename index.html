INGREDIENTS

  • Instant Coffee
  • Sugar
  • Hot Water
  • Milk

Dalgona Making Process

STEP 1 : Combine the sugar , instant coffee and hot water.
Add 2 tbsp of instant coffee, 2 tbsp of sugar and 2 tbsp of hot water to a medium mixing bowl.
STEP 2 : Wisk, wisk and wisk until the coffee is light and fluffy.
Using an electrc hand held mixer, whipthe coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when whisk is removed.
STEP 3 : Serve it with milk
Use a spoon to garnish a cup of hot or cold milk with coffee cream, serve it with a straw or a spoon, and stir, stir, stir to Enjoy!
